The changing weather

Rolling mean of your cycle length (line) wrapped in ±1 standard deviation (the shaded band), across six-cycle windows. Terracotta dots along the top mark ≥7-day jumps between consecutive cycles — the marker used in the STRAW+10 staging of the menopausal transition, which announces itself in variability before anything else. A widening band, or dots gathering where they used to be scattered, is that weather forming — seen early, calmly, on your own instrument.
